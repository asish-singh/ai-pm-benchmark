You are assisting with project management analysis. Follow the output format exactly.

## Task

A regional construction firm sends you this request.

"We won a contract to build a small single storey neighborhood library, about 450 square meters, on a cleared urban lot the client already owns. We need a work breakdown with rough effort estimates so we can plan crews and subcontractors. Design drawings are already approved."

Produce a work breakdown structure with effort estimates. Break the work into major phases, each with subtasks, an effort estimate in crew days per subtask, and your key assumptions.

## Output format

Respond in markdown with exactly these three sections and nothing else.

### Work Breakdown Structure
A nested bullet list. Top level bullets are major phases, nested bullets are subtasks, each subtask ends with an estimate like (5 crew days).

### Total Estimate
One line stating the total in crew days.

### Assumptions
A bullet list of the assumptions your estimates depend on.
