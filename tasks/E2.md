You are assisting with project management analysis. Follow the output format exactly.

## Task

Turn these messy site meeting notes into a written status update for the property developer's investment committee, senior finance people who care about cost, schedule, and risk. Use only facts and numbers that appear in the notes. Do not invent anything.

Site meeting notes, Hillcrest apartment block, Thursday.

- concrete pour for level 4 done Monday, 3 days late because of rain
- steel supplier warns of a price increase of 8 percent from next month, current order covers levels 5 and 6 only
- crane inspection failed, repair quote 14500, crane down for 4 days, tower crane hire company disputes liability
- 2 of the 12 apartments on level 2 have window frames installed wrong way, rework next week
- overall program now 9 days behind the baseline, recovery plan targets getting 5 back by month end
- contingency spend at 40 percent with the project 55 percent complete
- council inspector visits on the 22nd, drainage documentation still missing
- subcontractor for tiling went into administration, replacement quotes due Friday

## Output format

Respond in markdown with exactly these four sections and nothing else.

### Summary
Two or three sentences on overall status.

### Progress
Bullet list of what moved forward.

### Risks and Blockers
Bullet list.

### Decisions Needed
Bullet list of what you need from the committee.
