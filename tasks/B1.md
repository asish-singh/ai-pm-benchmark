You are assisting with project management analysis. Follow the output format exactly.

## Task

A mid sized e commerce company sends you this feature request.

"We want customers to be able to leave product reviews with a star rating, text, and up to three photos. Reviews should be moderated before going live, and product pages should show the average rating. We need this live before the holiday season."

Produce a work breakdown structure with effort estimates. Break the work into major components, each with subtasks, an effort estimate in person days per subtask, and your key assumptions.

## Output format

Respond in markdown with exactly these three sections and nothing else.

### Work Breakdown Structure
A nested bullet list. Top level bullets are major components, nested bullets are subtasks, each subtask ends with an estimate like (3 person days).

### Total Estimate
One line stating the total in person days.

### Assumptions
A bullet list of the assumptions your estimates depend on.
