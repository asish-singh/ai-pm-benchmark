You are assisting with project management analysis. Follow the output format exactly.

## Task

Read this project brief and identify the most important risks, each with a practical mitigation.

"BrightPath Media is launching a national rebrand campaign for a retail bank, going live across TV, out of home, and digital on 3 March. The bank's legal team must approve every asset but has quoted a 15 working day review turnaround, and the creative schedule assumes 5. The campaign relies on a celebrity spokesperson whose contract is signed but who has a history of public controversies. The TV spot will be filmed in a coastal location in February, the region's storm season. The media buy was committed and paid before the creative concept was tested with customers, and early focus group feedback on the concept was mixed. The client's CMO, the campaign's sponsor, is leaving the bank at the end of January. Three agencies are collaborating on delivery with no agreed decision making process between them. The campaign microsite is being built by an offshore team on a fixed bid, and the requirements are still changing weekly."

Identify 8 to 12 risks. For each risk give one sentence describing it and one sentence of mitigation.

## Output format

Respond in markdown with exactly this section and nothing else.

### Risks
A numbered list. Each entry has the form, **Risk:** one sentence. **Mitigation:** one sentence.
