You are assisting with project management analysis. Follow the output format exactly.

## Task

Read this project brief and identify the most important risks, each with a practical mitigation.

"Riverside Logistics is replacing its 15 year old warehouse management system with a new cloud platform. Go live is fixed for 1 November because the old vendor's support contract ends that day and will not be renewed. The internal team has never run a cloud migration before, and the one engineer who understands the legacy database schema is retiring in September. Data must be migrated from a undocumented custom SQL database. The new vendor is a startup founded 18 months ago, and the contract has no service level agreement yet. Warehouse staff, average tenure 12 years, will move from keyboard driven green screens to tablets, and no training budget has been approved. The project budget was set by the CFO before the vendor was selected, and the vendor's quote arrived 20 percent above it. Peak shipping season starts mid November, two weeks after go live."

Identify 8 to 12 risks. For each risk give one sentence describing it and one sentence of mitigation.

## Output format

Respond in markdown with exactly this section and nothing else.

### Risks
A numbered list. Each entry has the form, **Risk:** one sentence. **Mitigation:** one sentence.
